*** PLAYER ***
The overview for the player character.


SKILLS
-----------------
Here is a list of the skills the player can advance in the game.

Pilot:
If the player can fly a ship. The higher the level the more options there are to fly/drive.

Navigation:
The higher the navigation level the player can fly faster.

Unarmed:
The player can fight with fists really well. Multiple attacks per hit.

Farming:
The higher the level, the higher the crop output.

Small Arms:
How well the player can use small arms.

Medical:
The higher the level the better the player can heal themselves/others.

Hacking:
Effects how easy it is to hack systems.

Engineering:
Effects repairing the ship.

Electronics:
Effects the player's ability to make electronics.
