*** EQUIPMENT ***
Player's/party's equipment for the game.

Structure:
This is the structure of the dictionary for the equipment using nude as the example.
Finder: NUDE (how the equipment is found in the array)
Name: Naked (the name of the equipment listed)
Frame: 0 (reference frame for the body)
Type: Unequipped (the type such as: unequiped, light armor, heavy armor, ect.)
AC: 0 (the armor class to add to the player's existing armor class)
